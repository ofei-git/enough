Design Notes

Philosophy
Awareness, not restriction. The app shows you a mirror‚Äîwhere money went‚Äîwithout judgment. No red colors, no "overspent" shame. Just clarity.
The Ritual
Weekly review is the core habit. Import ‚Üí categorize unknowns ‚Üí see your story. Should feel like journaling, not accounting. 10 minutes max.
Visual Language
Warm stone tones, not cold finance blues. Sage green for "on track" (growth, not success). Amber for attention (not warning). No red anywhere.
Interactions
One-tap to accept suggestions. Swipe gestures for speed. "Always categorize this way" creates rules from corrections. Progress feels satisfying.
