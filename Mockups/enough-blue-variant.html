Color Comparison Notes

Sage feels more organic and grounded. It has associations with growth, balance, and nature. It's distinctly different from typical finance apps (which tend toward blue). The warmth pairs naturally with the cream canvas.

Dusty Blue is more classic and perhaps more universally "safe." It's sophisticated without being corporate. However, it edges closer to conventional finance app territory, even if muted.

The recommendation remains Sage. It's more distinctive, more aligned with the "enough" philosophy (growth, sufficiency, balance), and creates stronger differentiation from competitors like Copilot. The warmth of sage paired with cream feels more lifestyle/wellness, less finance.

But both work. If you find the blue more appealing personally, it's a valid choice. The design system, typography, and interactions are what make the app feel premium—the color is accent.
